Classlink

Classlink is a portal for students to access apps and accounts from one place easily. Students also use Classlink to log onto their Chromebooks.

How To Log Onto Classlink

Classlink Login Page

To log onto Classlink, enter your Classlink username and password and press sign in. Your username should be formated as: "first name initial" + "last name inital" + "student ID". Example: AA1234567. Your password initially will be automatically set by the school, and you will be required to change it to something new. Your password can not have your name in it.

This is what Classlink should look like when you log in.

Forgot Your Password

To reset your password, click "Help, I forgot my password".

Enter your Classlink username.

Answer the recovery question you have set up.

Enter a new password for your account, and you can now log back into your account with the new password. Your password can not have your name in it.

If you forgot your recovery question, try closing out of the page and going back in to get a different question. If you forgot all your questions, please talk to someone in your schools IT department or the front desk.

Changing App Passwords

After you log in, click your profile at the top right of the screen

Click the settings button.

Go to "App Passwords" and click on the pencil icon of the app you want to change the password of.

Click on the pencil icon of the password account you want to change. It will probably say "Default," choose that one.

From here you can change the password of the account or username information. To save your changes, press the "Save" button.
